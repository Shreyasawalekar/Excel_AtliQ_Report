📊 AtliQ Sales Report

An Excel-based sales analytics report for AtliQ Hardware, covering net sales performance across customers and markets from 2019 to 2021.

📁 Report Contents

1. Customer Performance Report:
   
Tracks net sales (in INR) for 50+ customers — including Amazon, Flipkart, Croma, Walmart, and AtliQ Exclusive — across 2019, 2020, and 2021, along with YoY growth (2021 vs 2020). Total net sales grew from ₹87.5M (2019) → ₹196.7M (2020) → ₹598.9M (2021).

2. Market Performance vs Target:
   
Compares 2021 actual sales against targets across 22 countries (India, USA, South Korea, Philippines, and more). Highlights the gap and percentage deviation per market. All markets fell short of their 2021 targets, with the overall shortfall at approximately -9.17%.

🔑 Key Metrics

Currency: Indian Rupees (INR)
Time Period: FY 2019 – 2021
Scope: Global — covers markets across Asia, Europe, America, and Oceania
Total 2021 Revenue: ₹598.9 Million
